“ART WORKERS“

Book design for Art Workers, a publication initiated by a group of art workers from Helsinki, Stockholm and Tallin.

Art Workers – Material Conditions and Labour Struggles in Contemporary Art Practice” presents case studies from the local art contexts of Estonia, Finland and Sweden, collects artist-testimonies, discusses activist practices and maps out contemporary and historical forms of organising within the international art field.

The Art Workers identity typography is a grotesk, a typeface originating from industrialism, combined with the digitally manipulated DIY aesthetics of our time. The typography reflects the urgency of handmade signage made for protesting, and nods towards the working class movement in the beginning of industralisation.

Available to purchase in selected book shops around Europe such as Lugemik Bookshop in Estonia, Konst/ig Books in Stockholm and ICA-Institute of Contemporary Art, London. It was also displayed at Offprint book fair at the Tate Modern in London in 2015.
